Description and Learning objectives

DHC5037 is one of the most popular courses in SAIHST. Currently, in clinical fields, there are growing interests in introducing digital technologies into the clinical environment. It has become important to assess patients' behavior involving digital health and to ensure good clinical interventions for demonstrating the robust clinical efficacy of digital health products. Thus, this course addresses theoretical approaches and methodologies for implementing clinical interventions in the context of health behavior changes. This course will introduce key terms and concepts of various health behavior theories which explain individual, interpersonal, and envrionmental factors. This course would be a meaningful challenge for the students who want to design an effective intervention for health behavior change or want to discuss health behavior changes in depth.


Along the way will students learn to

  • explore clinical and social impacts on target health behavior;
  • describe unmet needs for health behavior change;
  • distinguish each key concept between health behavior theories;
  • interpret the unmet needs based on the theories
  • design proper intervention strategies using the theories;
  • apply proper evaluation to figure out the efficacy of the intervention.

Exercises and Lab

All students will be expected to participate in 3 exercies. The exercises are small group activities, which are operated after finishing the theory part of each class (only for HBM, TTM, and SCT). Every class prepares 2 or 3 different health behaviors, students can join the break-out rooms in which you are interested. In the activities, you may complete short assignments to apply the theories you've learned to the target behavior.


Lab is an individual project instead of a Mid-term exam. You have to prepare a presentation regarding the needs assessment to change target health behavior in which you are currently intersted. Please note that the presentation should include the reason for choosing the target behavior, the theory to explain the target behavior, and the unmet needs of the target behavior based on the chosen theory, within 5 slides except for the title and references. You don't need to conduct field studies, you just prepare with reviewing of the published literatures related to your interests.

Final project

Final project: Designing a health behavior change interventions

All students are require to design a health behavior change interventions with using digital technologies. You can choose a health behavior which has significant social or clincial problems in your community. Then, you should describe what factors are associated the unhealth behavior based on health behavior theories. Finally, you should present intervention strategies to solve the factors explained in the previous step, along with the proper evaluation method.


The following component should be included in your presentation

  • Expected outcomes (social, clinical, or behavioral) of the target health behavior
  • Factors associated with the target behavior (individual, interpersonal, and environmental)
  • Intervention strategies with using digital technologies
  • Evaluation methods for figuring out the efficacy of the designed intervention

Conducting a field study such as a qualitative interview or quantitative survey or pilot study of designed intervention is not mandatory but preferred. We will give advantages to the student who tried any of them, even if the sample size was small. Additionally, you can ask teaching assistants to help for preparing the final project. Don't hesitate to contact them.
